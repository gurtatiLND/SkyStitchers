Let's Get Ready

Here is a guide to getting started.

Step 1: Assemble Basic Tools

Find the tools and materials

To get started with any sewing project you will need these essentials:

Thread
One or more spools - pick the colours you like!
Needle
Choose one with a bigger eye for easier threading
Fabric
Check project material list for details.
A box
To keep your tools and materials - a shoebox will do!
All tools ready!

Step 2: Find a Space

A good space to work will have:

Good light
It could be near a window, a well-lit room, or near a desk-light
A table
A worktop, surface or floor to lay out your materials. A place that doesn't block a walkway or the stairs.
Your space is ready!
